// Trainings We Provide

Explore The Trainings Of Our Institute

Bakery Basic

Basic bakery training provides foundational knowledge and skills, covering essential techniques like dough preparation, baking fundamentals, and basic pastry production.

Bakery Advanced

Advanced bakery training builds upon this foundation, delving into complex recipes, advanced baking methods, specialized pastry arts, and advanced decorating techniques. It also includes in-depth knowledge of ingredient selection, recipe development, and creative innovation in the bakery industry.

Barista

Barista training is a comprehensive program that equips aspiring baristas with essential skills in coffee-making, including espresso extraction, latte art, and customer service. It covers coffee origins, brewing methods, and workspace cleanliness to create exceptional coffee experiences for customers.

Italian Food

Italian Food Training offers an immersive experience in mastering traditional Italian culinary techniques, including pasta making, pizza crafting, sandwich, and hot dog preparations. Italian cuisine captivates with its rich flavors, fresh ingredients, and authenticity, known for dishes like spaghetti, lasagna, and iconic pizzas.
